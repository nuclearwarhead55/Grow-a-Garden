Early Game Guide

Welcome to Grow a Garden! Here's a quick start:

  1. Go to the **Shop**.
  2. Buy 2 Carrots with the $20 you start with.
  3. Plant and grow them.
  4. Once grown, **Sell** the Carrots.
  5. You should now have $30. Go back to the **Shop**.
  6. Buy 3 Carrots.
  7. Plant, grow, and sell them.
  8. Continue this process of reinvesting until you feel ready for the Mid Game guide.

If you encounter problems, check the (future) Bugs/Troubleshooting section. For help deciding what to buy next, see the (future) Seeds section.
